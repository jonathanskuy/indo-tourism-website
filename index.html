Tangerang Regency

Experience the modern pulse of Tangerang Regency through two of its most vibrant landmarks — AEON Mall BSD, a haven for shopping, dining, and family entertainment, and Indomilk Arena, the beating heart of sports and live events in the region. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway, a shopping spree, or to cheer on your favorite team, Tangerang Regency offers an energetic urban retreat just minutes from Jakarta.

AEON Mall BSD

AEON Mall BSD City is one of Tangerang Regency’s most popular lifestyle and entertainment destinations. Blending modern architecture with a distinctly Japanese atmosphere, this mall offers a refreshing shopping experience like no other in the region.

From high-end fashion outlets, electronics stores, and beauty brands to everyday essentials, the mall caters to all ages and interests. It is especially well-known for its Japanese-style food court, where visitors can enjoy sushi, ramen, takoyaki, and more — all in a beautifully themed setting.

Families and children will love the variety of entertainment options, including game arcades, a modern cinema, and seasonal cultural events. The mall also hosts the largest Daiso store in Indonesia, drawing shoppers from all over Greater Jakarta.

Whether you're coming for retail therapy, a casual stroll, or an all-day outing with family and friends, AEON Mall BSD offers a complete lifestyle experience that reflects the energy and diversity of Tangerang's urban growth.

Indomilk Arena

Indomilk Arena stands as a proud symbol of Tangerang Regency’s growing sports and entertainment scene. Located in the center of BSD City, this modern stadium is home to the beloved Liga 1 football team, Persita Tangerang.

With a seating capacity of over 30,000, Indomilk Arena regularly hosts high-energy football matches, attracting thousands of passionate fans. The stadium is equipped with modern lighting, seating, and facilities, making it a top-tier venue for both national and regional competitions.

Beyond football, Indomilk Arena is also a venue for concerts, festivals, and community events that bring people together from all across Banten and Jakarta. Its convenient location, ample parking, and proximity to malls, hotels, and public transport make it a key hub for large-scale gatherings.

Whether you're cheering in the stands, attending a music event, or simply exploring the area, Indomilk Arena offers a vibrant and unifying space that reflects the local pride and spirit of Tangerang.

Scientia Square Park

Scientia Square Park (SQP) is a vibrant open-air green space located in the heart of Gading Serpong, Tangerang Regency. It offers a refreshing blend of nature and recreation, making it a go-to spot for families, students, and city dwellers seeking a break from urban life. With lush gardens, koi ponds, and rice fields, the park feels like a hidden oasis surrounded by the modern cityscape.

The park is designed with interactivity in mind. Children and adults alike can enjoy outdoor activities like skateboarding, wall climbing, and biking, while younger visitors can participate in educational mini-farming and butterfly dome experiences. These features make SQP both a fun and enriching destination for all ages.

Scientia Square Park also plays an important role in promoting eco-awareness. It regularly hosts sustainability events, eco-workshops, and cultural activities that bring the community together. Its dedication to green living makes it more than just a park — it's a learning space and environmental hub.

Whether you're visiting for a morning jog, a relaxing picnic, or simply to reconnect with nature, Scientia Square Park offers a calm, beautiful, and refreshing retreat right in the middle of Tangerang’s thriving BSD area.

Why Visit These Places?

AEON Mall BSD, Indomilk Arena, and Scientia Square Park aren’t just locations — they’re immersive experiences that showcase the modern, dynamic, and green lifestyle of Tangerang Regency. Each destination offers something unique for families, urban explorers, and weekend adventurers.

Diverse Entertainment

Enjoy everything from Japanese dining and global fashion at AEON Mall BSD to arcades, cinemas, and family fun zones — all under one roof.

Nature Meets Activity

Scientia Square Park blends lush greenery with interactive outdoor experiences. Walk among rice fields, feed koi fish, or join a community eco-event — it’s a perfect mix of relaxation and recreation.

Live Sports & Easy Access

Catch thrilling football matches and community festivals at Indomilk Arena, home of Persita Tangerang. All three spots are easily reachable by car or public transport and offer excellent parking and facilities.
